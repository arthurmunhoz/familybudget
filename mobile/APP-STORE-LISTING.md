## Description

> Run your whole home from one app — not five.
>
> One Roof brings your family's calendar, shopping list, budget, pets, documents, and everyday coordination into a single shared space. Everyone in the household sees the same thing, in sync, instantly.
>
> • WHEREABOUTS — see your family on a live map with drive-time ETAs, get an alert when someone arrives at or leaves home, school, or work, and set a safe zone around anyone. Sharing is opt-in and off by default, visible only to your household.
> • SHARED CALENDAR — everyone's plans in one place, color-coded by person, with birthdays, anniversaries, and renewal reminders.
> • SHOPPING LIST — add items and watch them sync live across the family; check things off from anywhere.
> • MONEY — track spending by budget and period. Snap a photo of a receipt and it fills in the amount, date, and category for you.
> • PET CARE — vet visits, medications, and vaccine due dates, with reminders so nothing slips.
> • DOCUMENTS — keep IDs, insurance, and records in a Face ID–locked vault.
> • FAMILY — everyone's key info at a glance.
> • NUDGES — one tap to say "on my way," "dinner's ready," or "grab milk" — and a call button for "need a hand."
>
> Built for real families:
> • One shared household — invite everyone, one simple bill (no per-person upcharge).
> • Private by design — your data is only visible to your household. No ads. No tracking.
> • Works in English, Spanish, and Portuguese.
>
> One roof. One app. Everything your household needs to run smoothly.

### NEW for v1.2 — required because of Whereabouts (location). Add these or risk rejection:
- **Why background location ("Always"):** One Roof lets family members share their
  location with their own household so they can see each other on a map and get
  arrival/departure alerts (e.g. "Emma arrived at School"). Background/Always
  access is needed so geofence arrival alerts fire when the app isn't open. This
  is core to the feature, not advertising or tracking.
- **It's opt-in and off by default:** sharing starts OFF; the user turns it on in
  Whereabouts and can pause it anytime. Location is visible only to the user's own
  household and is never sold or used for ads/tracking.
- **How to test it:** the live family map needs at least TWO members sharing. To
  see it with one reviewer device, the demo household is pre-seeded with a second
  member already sharing a location. (Set this up before submitting, or note that
  a single account will see the map with just themselves.)
- **Plus (in-app purchase) features:** unlimited saved Places and unlimited Safety
  Radius time are One Roof Plus. The free tier includes one Place and 30 min of
  Safety Radius per day, so the feature is testable without buying. Use a sandbox
  account to test the Plus upgrade if desired.

### App Privacy (App Store Connect → App Privacy) — MUST update for v1.2

  the user's identity: **Yes** (tied to their household account). Used for
  **tracking: No**. This is the one nutrition-label change this release requires —
  everything else (receipts→Claude, contact info, user content) is unchanged.
- Confirm the Privacy Policy page describes location sharing before you submit.
