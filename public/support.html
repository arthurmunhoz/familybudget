Getting started

Sign in with Apple or Google. One person creates the household and invites the rest of the family. Everyone then shares the same calendar, shopping list, budget, pets, documents and nudges.

Common questions

How do I add someone to my household?
The household owner can invite members; invited members sign in and join automatically.

Is my data private?
Yes — your household's data is only visible to your household's members. See our Privacy Policy.

How does receipt scanning work?
In Money, tap the scan button and photograph a receipt; the details are extracted for you to review before saving. It's optional — you can always type entries by hand.

How do I turn on reminders?
Settings → Enable notifications, then allow notifications when prompted.

How do I delete my account?
Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your account and personal data (and the whole household if you're the last member).
